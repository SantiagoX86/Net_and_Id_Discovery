## 1. Purpose

This Functional Requirements Document (FRD) defines the functional capabilities and expected behaviors of the Enterprise Security Discovery Orchestration Framework.

The system is designed to perform agentless, non-exploitative discovery of externally observable network and identity exposure from a low-privilege vantage point.

---

## 2. Scope

The system shall provide the ability to:

- Discover network-accessible services on a target host
- Identify externally exposed identity and authentication services
- Normalize findings into a consistent, structured format
- Produce machine-readable and human-readable output artifacts

The system shall operate without credentials and without modifying the target environment.

---

## 3. Functional Overview

The framework consists of:

- Core orchestration layer
- Network Discovery Domain
- Identity Discovery Domain
- Output & Reporting module

Each component contributes to the overall discovery and reporting workflow.

---

## 4. Functional Requirements

### 4.1 Core Framework Requirements

- The system shall initialize an execution context for each run
- The system shall coordinate execution of one or more discovery domains
- The system shall ensure failure in one domain does not terminate overall execution
- The system shall aggregate findings from all domains

---

### 4.2 Network Discovery Requirements

- The system shall identify reachable TCP services using a predefined port list
- The system shall determine service exposure based on TCP connectivity
- The system shall avoid protocol-level interaction beyond connection establishment
- The system shall generate findings for detected network services

---

### 4.3 Identity Discovery Requirements

- The system shall identify externally exposed identity and authentication services
- The system shall detect identity-related service exposure using TCP connectivity
- The system shall not perform authentication, credential transmission, or directory queries
- The system shall generate findings representing identity-related exposure signals
- The system shall operate in a non-exploitative and minimally interactive manner

---

### 4.4 Output and Reporting Requirements

- The system shall produce JSON output containing all findings
- The system shall generate Markdown reports for human consumption
- The system shall include evidence and timestamps in all findings

---

## 5. Data Requirements

Each finding shall include:

- domain
- category
- target
- evidence
- confidence
- observed_at (UTC timestamp)

All data must conform to the normalized finding schema.

---

## 6. Execution Requirements

- The system shall execute discovery domains sequentially
- The system shall maintain deterministic ordering of operations
- The system shall enforce bounded timeouts for all network operations

---

## 7. Security Requirements

The system shall:

- Operate without credentials
- Avoid authentication attempts
- Avoid exploitation techniques
- Avoid brute-force behavior
- Avoid modifying target system state

---

## 8. Constraints

- The system shall operate using standard OS-level networking APIs
- The system shall not use raw packet crafting
- The system shall maintain deterministic behavior across runs
- The system shall preserve auditability of all findings

---

## 9. Acceptance Criteria

The system shall be considered functionally complete when:

- All required discovery domains execute successfully
- Findings are generated and normalized correctly
- Output artifacts are produced as defined
- Execution is deterministic under identical conditions
